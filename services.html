Student Visa in Japan

The Student Visa allows international students to study in Japan at various educational institutions, such as language schools. This visa requires students to be enrolled in a recognized program and meet specific financial and academic requirements.

SSW Visa

The SSW Visa is a program introduced by the Japanese government to address labor shortages in various industries. It allows foreign workers with specific skills and expertise to work in Japan for an extended period. This visa offers a pathway for skilled workers to gain employment in sectors such as agriculture, construction, etc.

Training Visa in Japan

The Training Visa allows foreign nationals to undergo training in Japan to acquire skills and techniques that can be difficult to obtain in their home country. This visa is aimed at individuals who will participate in training programs provided by Japanese companies, organizations, or other entities.
